Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

To place an order on FoodNest, first select your delivery location, choose a restaurant, browse their menu, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to sign in or create an account if you don't have one, and then enter your payment details to complete the order.

FoodNest accepts various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay, and cash on delivery in selected areas. You can save your preferred payment methods in your account for faster checkout.

Once your order is confirmed, you can track it in real-time through the FoodNest app or website. Go to "My Orders" in your account to see the status of your current order. You'll receive updates when the restaurant accepts your order, when it's being prepared, when it's on its way, and when it's delivered.

If you encounter any issues with your order, you can report it through the "Help" section in your account. Alternatively, you can contact our customer support team directly via phone or email. We aim to resolve all issues promptly and ensure your satisfaction with our service.
